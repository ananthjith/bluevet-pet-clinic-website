Compassionate Care Since 2015

Blu Vet Pet Hospital was founded with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality veterinary care while treating every pet as if they were our own. Located in the heart of Kochi, we have grown to become one of Kerala's most trusted pet care facilities.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology, and our team of experienced veterinarians and caring staff work together to ensure your pet receives the best possible care.
